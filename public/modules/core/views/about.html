About Us

Mission

Get Well Africa is a healthcare initiative.

Goals and Mission

Bridge gap in the healthcare outcomes between developed nations and Africa through:

  • Medical eduation of personnel (physicians, nurses, community health workers, traditional headlers, and administrators)
  • Donation of equipment nad supplies to support their network
  • Exchange program
  • Building collaborative community health centers focused primarily on preventative medicine

Conduct community health fairs focused on issues such as:

  • Vaccination
  • Hypertension
  • Malnutrition
  • Reproductive health (including cancer and STI screenings)

Perform research to help guide the direction and focus of the initiatives
